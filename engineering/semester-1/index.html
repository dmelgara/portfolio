First semester of the MET Advanced Diploma. Covers the foundations of engineering drawing and CAD, manufacturing systems, workplace legislation, and the applied mathematics underpinning engineering calculations.

Technical Drawing

Fundamentals of multiview orthographic projection using AutoCAD. Covers drafting standards, dimensioning, tolerancing, section views, and the full workflow from freehand sketch to finished CAD drawing.

Introduction to manufacturing systems, production planning, quality control, and lean principles. Examines how products move from raw material to finished good in industrial environments.

About This Portfolio

Academic work,
documented honestly

This section of my website documents the academic work I'm completing as part of the Mechanical Engineering Technology (MET) Advanced Diploma at Mohawk College. Each course page is a case-study-style record of what I studied, what I built or produced, and what I took away from it — not a transcript, but a working log of the learning.

The portfolio is structured by semester and scales as I progress through the program. Right now it covers Semester 1. Future semesters will be added as I complete them. The long-term goal is for this section to tell the story of how I move from foundational coursework at Mohawk through to an engineering degree and into the nuclear industry.
